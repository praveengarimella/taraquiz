General Competency Test

Welcome to the Home Page of the General Competency Test . The purpose of this test is to evaluate your PUC level skills. The competency test consists of 3 components:

I. Aptitude Test
II. English Language Comprehension Test
III. Telugu Language Comprehension Test

Aptitude test is for 30 minutes and evaluates your quantitative, analytical, reasoning and verbal skills.

English Language comprehension Test is for 25 minutes and evaluates your capability of reading, listening, speaking and writing in English.

Telugu Language Comprehension Test is for 15 minutes and evaluates your capability of reading and listening skills in Telugu.

All the questions of this test carry 1 mark, except 2 essay writing questions which carry 6 marks each and 1 English speaking question that carries 4 marks. Total Marks: 50, Total Time: 70 Minutes
